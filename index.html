üè† The Flat Booking Analogy

Imagine you want to book a flat for ‚Çπ50 lakh. You pay a ‚Çπ1 lakh booking amount today to lock in that price for the next 6 months. If the flat price rises to ‚Çπ70 lakh, you still buy at ‚Çπ50 lakh. If it falls, you walk away ‚Äî only losing ‚Çπ1 lakh.

That ‚Çπ1 lakh booking amount is the option premium. This app calculates exactly that ‚Äî for Indian stocks.

üé≤ How Monte Carlo works

The computer rolls a virtual dice 10,000 times, each time simulating a different possible future for the stock. Each coloured line on the chart is one possible future. The app then averages all outcomes to find the fair price. The more dice rolls (paths), the more accurate the answer.

üßÆ Black-Scholes

A Nobel Prize-winning formula (1973) that solves the same problem in milliseconds using pure maths. The Monte Carlo result should be very close to this ‚Äî you can see the difference in the price card above.
